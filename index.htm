Biography

I received my B.S. degree from College of Electric and Information Engineering, Southwest University in 2016. I am now a Ph.D. student at the Department of Software, Tsinghua University. I work in the Systems and Ubiquitous Networking (SUN) group in the Institute of Trustworthy Networks and Systems, advised by Yuan He. My research interests include wireless networking and ubiquitous computing.

Research Interests
  • Wireless networking: Cross-technology communication; Wireless coexistence; Heterogeneous networking; Backscatter communication
  • Ubiquitous computing: Mobile sensing
